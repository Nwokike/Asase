Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

ASASE collects location data that you provide through our search interface. This information is used solely to generate environmental risk analyses for the specified locations.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Generate real-time environmental risk assessments
  • Create archived reports for public environmental intelligence
  • Improve our AI analysis algorithms
  • Provide location-specific environmental insights

Data Storage

Generated environmental reports are stored indefinitely as part of our public environmental intelligence archive. These reports contain location names, coordinates, environmental data, and AI-generated analyses.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap for geocoding services
  • OpenWeatherMap for weather data
  • NASA and Sentinel Hub for satellite imagery
  • Google Gemini for AI analysis

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our platform.
