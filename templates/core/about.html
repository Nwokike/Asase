About ASASE

Our Mission

ASASE (Earth Intelligence) is Africa's premier environmental intelligence platform, providing real-time, AI-driven risk analysis on critical environmental factors affecting communities across the continent.

Our focus on SDG 15: Life on Land drives our commitment to preserving and restoring terrestrial ecosystems while providing actionable insights to communities, policymakers, and researchers.

What We Analyze

Flood Risk Assessment

Real-time precipitation forecasting and terrain analysis to predict flood vulnerabilities.

Air Quality Monitoring

Comprehensive air quality assessment using multiple environmental indicators.

Land Health & SDG 15

Satellite-based vegetation analysis (NDVI) to track land degradation and ecosystem health.
